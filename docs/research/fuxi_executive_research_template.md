# Fuxi Executive Research Template (First 100 Days)

## Purpose
Capture C-level priorities, pain points, and decision patterns in the first 100 days to inform AI/UX and schema evolution.

## Survey Structure (7 questions)
1. **Top Decisions**: Which decisions are hardest in your first 100 days? (ranked list)
2. **Trusted Data Sources**: What data do you trust most today? (select + free text)
3. **Bandwidth Drains**: Which conversations consume most time? (multi-select)
4. **Risk/Opportunity**: Where do you see recurring risk vs opportunity? (free text + tags)
5. **Visualization Impact**: Would live visualization change your confidence? How? (scale + free text)
6. **Decision Quality**: How do you measure a “good decision”? (intuition / consensus / data) (scale)
7. **Blockers**: What blocks actionable tech strategy? (free text)

## Interview Guide (Deep-Dive)
- Opening: role, context, current transformation focus.
- Decision moments: last 3 critical decisions; data/inputs used.
- Confidence: what increases or erodes confidence?
- Dependencies: which teams/systems slow decisions?
- Success definition: what will make month 3 and month 6 a win?

## Workshop Pattern
- 60–90 min facilitation with prioritization matrix.
- Outputs: top 5 initiatives, risks, and data gaps.
- Export: Miro/whiteboard snapshot + AI summary.

## Empathy Loop
Listen → Translate → Visualize → Reflect → Evolve.

## Data Mapping Hints
- System/Integration gaps → Digital Enterprise map overlays.
- ROI & risk asks → ROI/Simulation layers.
- Confidence themes → AI Opportunity & KPI modules.

## Deliverables
- Survey JSON (`src/domain/research/templates/survey_100days.json`)
- Research page (`src/app/research/page.tsx`)
- Insights log (append findings + tags per respondent).
